Some Things About Me

Hello! My name is Luis Arroyo. I am a run-of-the-mill nerd who loves a fun mental challenge, working with his hands, and music. My aim is to become a medical device designer so that I may help improve current systems on the market. Outside of engineering, I would love to improve my 3D modeling to skills to create my own miniatures for board games.

I am a big fan of Sci-fi and fantasy genre's of media, mainly Star Wars, Warhammer 40K, Lord of the Rings, and Fallout. In my free time I have been working on a Death Korps of Krieg Kill Team and a Blood Angels 40K army, which has helped me learn more about painting and miniatures. I am also involved in a couple of Dungeons and Dragons campaigns. Whenever I am not playing with tiny plastic or dice, I can be found either playing an RPG game or practicing some instrument. I love spending time with my friends and taking the time to enjoy the small things in life.
